DETAILS

Information of how vehicle details work.

1) Vehicle Registration is the registration of a motor vehicle with a government authority, either compulsory or otherwise.

2) The purpose of vehicle registration is to establish a link between a vehicle and an owner or user of the vehicle.

3) The owner of the vehicle is the name of the person on whom the vehicle is registered as per the RTO records.

4) Without a valid registration certificate, you do not have the legal rights to sell the vehicle or transfer the ownership of the vehicle.

5) Transport Ministry of India keeps a track of the number of vehicles in the country and registering every vehicle helps them get the statistics right.

6) Vehicle Registration Number is a unique number(key) for every vehicle. No two vehicles can have the same number.

7) The Vehicle Registration Number has information about the state and area of purchase.

8) With the Vehicle Registration Number alone we can find all the information about the owner and the vehicle owned.
